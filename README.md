# Turbo Edition (WIP)
## About
Turbo Edition is a **work in progress** Risk of Rain 2 mod, a kind of personal project to learn Unity and programming, that unlike SS2, **will _not_ be a direct port** of RoR1's Turbo Edition.
The plans are to spice up the game a bit, with new content or rebalanced / changed content from RoR1's Turbo Edition, including support (*and maybe exclusive content!*) if Starstorm 2 is installed.

## Goals
Current short-term goals are to just get the current content (*At the moment of writing, 3 equipment, 3 common items, 2 uncommon items, 1 legendary, 1 lunar, and a artifact*)  to have **models and _networking_**.
Down the road, the ideas, in order of relevance are:
- 1 Common Item, 3 Uncommon Items, 2 Legendary Items, 2 Lunar Items
- *2 Survivors*
- 3 Artifacts
- 3 New Elite types
- A currency rework
- And whole new item tier

## Contribute
For now I'm working alone, but, if you are feeling like losing your sanity and self-respect by working with awful code, welcome!
I'm accepting literally any form of contribution as long as it fits the scope of the project, that means code reviews, code, assets, and so on.
